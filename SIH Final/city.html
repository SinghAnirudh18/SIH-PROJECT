Rajasthan

Rajasthan, located in the northwestern part of India, is the largest state by area in the country.
Known for its vibrant culture, rich history, and stunning architecture, Rajasthan is often referred to as the "Land of Kings" due to its royal heritage and numerous majestic forts and palaces. The state is a major tourist destination, attracting visitors with its colorful festivals, vast deserts, and unique traditions.
Rajasthan's capital, Jaipur, is part of the famous "Golden Triangle" tourist circuit, along with Delhi and Agra. The state is home to iconic landmarks like the Amber Fort, Mehrangarh Fort, and the City Palace. Udaipur, with its serene lakes and romantic ambiance, is known as the "Venice of the East," while Jaisalmer, situated in the heart of the Thar Desert, is famed for its golden sand dunes and camel safaris.
Rajasthan is also known for its vibrant handicrafts, textiles, and jewelry, reflecting the region’s artisanal heritage. The cuisine of Rajasthan is distinctive, with dishes like dal baati churma and gatte ki sabzi showcasing the state’s culinary traditions.
